What are NouCredits?

NouCredits are our very own currency, which you can earn by purchasing items! You spend these credits to purchase essentials. You also get a small amount of cashback, so you will never run out of credits! Very soon, we will be adding functionality to donate to help us keep taking Camp Nou forward.
